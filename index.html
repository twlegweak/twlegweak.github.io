Equilibrium.

Equilibrium requires that the algebraic sum of forces and moments on a system are equal to zero.
Mathematically, in a 2D system of foces, this requires:

\(\Sigma F_x =0\)     \(\Sigma F_y = 0\)      \(\Sigma M_o = 0\)
